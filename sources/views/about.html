About Me

My name is Pree Simphliphan, an Engineering Student at Boston University class of 2026. I major in Computer Engineering with a minor in Mechanical Engineering and concentration in Robotics.

I am a recipient of King's Scholarship awarded by Royal Thai Government for to pursue Bachelor's Degree abroad. Prior to Boston University, I studied one year of Postgraduate in Brewster Academy where I learned both academic and culture in American Education and adjust myself to be a innovative and creative student.

During my time at Boston University, I have exposed myself to various fields; after my freshmen year, when I took Engineering hands-on class and learned the way engineer thinks and develops products, I have decided to continue my journey as an engineer because I have enjoyed developing new products that are crucial and impactful to society.

With my curiosity in embedding software into hardware and applying highly-skilled knowledge, my interest area spans towards Microcontrollers, Smart Devices, IoT, Embedded Systems, Artificial Intelligence, Robotics
